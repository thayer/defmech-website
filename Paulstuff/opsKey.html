NOTE - Order of Operations does not apply. Use parenthesis.  
Operation Description
a+b Adds a plus b
a-b Subtracts a minus b
a*b Multiplies a times b
a/b Divides a by b
a^b Raises a to the b power
exp(a) Raises e to the a power
ln(a) Computes the natural logorithm of a
log(a) Computes the base 10 logorithm of a
sqrt(a) Computes the square root of a
sin(a) Sine of a
tan(a) Tangent of a
asin(a) Computes the principal value of the arc sine of a
acos(a) Computes the principal value of the arc cosine of a
atan(a) Computes the principal value of the arc tangent of a
sinh(a) Hyperbolic sine of a
tanh(a) Hyperbolic tangent of a
min(a,b,c,d,...) Takes the minimum of a list(2 or more) of expressions
max(a,b,c,d...) Takes the maximum of a list(2 or more) of expressions
